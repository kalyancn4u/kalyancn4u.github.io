#1 · FEYNMAN TECHNIQUE🎓 Feynman Technique
📖 “Explain [TOPIC] in simple terms, as if teaching a 10-year-old. Identify gaps in your understanding.”
🟢 Beginner ★ best🔵 Intermediate ★ best🟣 Advanced ★ best🟠 Quick revision (good)
#2 · SPACED REPETITION⏳ Spaced Repetition
🗓️ “Plan a schedule to revisit [TOPIC] across several days. Revisiting at spaced intervals reinforces long-term memory.”
🟢 Beginner ★ best🔵 Intermediate ★ best🟣 Advanced ★ best🟠 Quick revision ★ best
#3 · COLOR CODING🎨 Color Coding
🖍️ “Assign colors to each subtopic in your notes. Visual color cues differentiate and organize information for better recall.”
🟢 Beginner ★ best🔵 Intermediate (good)🟣 Advanced (less)🟠 Quick revision ★ best
#4 · MNEMONICS🧠 Mnemonics
🔤 “Create a mnemonic to remember key points. Turn complex information into a simple, memorable phrase.”
🟢 Beginner ★ best🔵 Intermediate (good)🟣 Advanced (less)🟠 Quick revision ★ best
#5 · MIND MAPPING🗺️ Mind Mapping
🌿 “Create a mind map of [TOPIC] with branches for each subtopic. Organize visually to grasp main ideas faster.”
🟢 Beginner ★ best🔵 Intermediate ★ best🟣 Advanced (good)🟠 Quick revision (good)
#6 · ANALOGIES🔗 Analogies
⚡ “Create 3 analogies to explain different parts of [TOPIC]. Compare unfamiliar ideas to familiar ones.”
🟢 Beginner ★ best🔵 Intermediate ★ best🟣 Advanced (good)🟠 Quick revision (less)
#7 · STORYTELLING📖 Storytelling
✨ “Turn key concepts into a short story. Narrative provides context to remember information effectively.”
🟢 Beginner ★ best🔵 Intermediate (good)🟣 Advanced (less)🟠 Quick revision (less)
#8 · WALK AND TALK🚶 Walk and Talk
🌳 “Take a walk while explaining [TOPIC] aloud. Moving while learning boosts energy, concentration, and memory.”
🟢 Beginner (good)🔵 Intermediate ★ best🟣 Advanced (good)🟠 Quick revision (good)
#9 · TEACH WITH ANALOGIES👩‍🏫 Teach with Analogies
🧸 “Create an analogy for each subtopic as if explaining to a younger sibling. Relate abstract concepts to something familiar.”
🟢 Beginner (good)🔵 Intermediate ★ best🟣 Advanced ★ best🟠 Quick revision (less)
#10 · RAPID NOTE-TAKING✍️ Rapid Note-Taking
⚡ “Take notes on [TOPIC] using only keywords and symbols. Focus on essentials to learn faster without details.”
🟢 Beginner (good)🔵 Intermediate ★ best🟣 Advanced ★ best🟠 Quick revision ★ best
